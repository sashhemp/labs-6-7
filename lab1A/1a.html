ALIEN STAGE

The plot of Alien Stage revolves around a reality audition program where pet humans are forced to compete on stage in front of a bunch of aliens. The losers of the competition face termination, while the winners get to survive. The story takes place in a dyst opian future where humans have lost their autonomy and are treated as entertainment for the alien overlords.
